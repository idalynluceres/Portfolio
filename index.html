ABOUT ME.

"Nothing worth having comes easy." ~Theodore Roosevelt

I can still remember how passionate I was in being in the Information Technology World during high school. However, life happens and I ended up taking a baccalaureate degree in Education. Don't get me wrong though, I also love the career I chose.

I managed getting my license year 2017, few months after I graduated. I landed a teaching job on a public school in my hometown. I stayed a few months after I decided to leave the province. After a month of staying in the city, I got accepted as a Customer Service Representative on one of the largest BPO company.

Being the optimistic person I am, I decided to pursue being in the BPO industry since I was already loving the environment I was in. From that day on, I accepted all opportunities that came my way in that field.

One day came, someone I know from before brought news to me that I can still pursue my dream career even if I was not able to get an IT degree. I managed to get myself in on this bootcamp (Kodego) and I can say that I am loving where I am in now. I am looking forward to being a full-stack web developer and share my knowledge and skills I gained all throughout the bootcamp.
